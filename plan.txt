Overall Vision :
To overall each feature in a way that makes the game more fun for me to play.
This will include the creation of new civilizations, units, buildings, policies,
techs, and beliefs. It will also include making adjustments to existing things.
I will also create a new economic victory condition.

I will want to eventually edit all the civilizations I can to buff them and make them far
unique.

Policy Adjustments :
Different Ideologies will include: Freedom, Authoritarian, Globalist, Theocratic
The ideologies will have focus on different victory conditions.  Freedom will lend itself
to the new economic victory as well as secondarily to a cultural and scientific victory.
Authoritarian will lend itself primarily to a domination victory, and it could maybe
be used for a scientific victory.  The Globalist will lend itself to a diplomacy victory,
with a secondary focus on scientific.  The Theocratic will primarily focus on a religious
victory, with a secondary focus on cultural.

Freedom :
 - Free Market (Economic buildings provide an additional +1 gold.)
 - Liberalism (Scientific buildings provide +1 culture.)
 - Freedom of Information 
 - Patriotism
 - Constitution
 - Educational Competition
